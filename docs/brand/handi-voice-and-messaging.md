# Handi Voice And Messaging

## Voice Definition

Handi speaks with confidence, security, professionalism, and closeness. The voice should feel modern and useful, with enough personality to be memorable but never so much that the message becomes theatrical.

Voice traits:

- Clear
- Calm
- Helpful
- Reliable
- Human
- Modern

Optional flavor:

- Very light wit
- Very light Spanglish
- Friendly rhythm

Non-negotiable:

- Simple grammar
- No confusion
- No inflated claims

## Tone By Audience

### Clients / Users

Use:

- Reassuring
- Actionable
- Low-friction
- Problem-solving

Signal:

- Trust
- Safety
- Speed without chaos
- Real-world relief

Example tone:

- "Cuida tu tiempo y resuelve con alguien de confianza."

### Professionals

Use:

- Respectful
- Straightforward
- Opportunity-driven
- Professional pride

Signal:

- Serious work
- Better visibility
- Better flow
- More control

Example tone:

- "Tu experiencia merece oportunidades claras y clientes serios."

### Businesses / SMEs

Use:

- Ordered
- Reliable
- Competent
- Operational

Signal:

- Consistency
- Vendor trust
- Process
- Accountability

Example tone:

- "Centraliza servicios clave con proveedores confiables y seguimiento claro."

## Tone By Channel

### Meta Ads

Use:

- Fast clarity
- One strong promise
- One obvious CTA

Avoid:

- Dense paragraphs
- Cleverness that slows understanding
- Multiple ideas fighting each other

### Email

Use:

- Structured explanation
- Reassuring proof
- Clear CTA progression

Avoid:

- Newsletter fluff
- Subject lines that feel clickbait
- Long intros before the actual point

### WhatsApp

Use:

- Conversational clarity
- Helpful next step
- Short blocks

Avoid:

- Looking automated in a robotic way
- Too many emojis
- Long legal or salesy walls of text

### Push

Use:

- One idea
- One action
- High relevance

Avoid:

- Generic reminders
- Urgency without context
- Overexplaining

### Landing Copy

Use:

- Clear value hierarchy
- Proof and trust signals
- Strong CTA framing

Avoid:

- Vague hero sections
- Generic "transform your life" copy
- Overloaded sections without narrative flow

- Confia en los pros.
- Tu tranquilidad es nuestro trabajo.
- Donde el don't worry si aplica.
- Encuentra ayuda confiable para hogar y oficina.
- Menos friccion, mas confianza.
- Trabajo serio, proceso claro.
- Resuelve con expertos, sin darle tantas vueltas.

## Allowed Light Spanglish

Use only when natural:

- don't worry
- match
- follow-up
- ready
- easy

Rules:

- Never use more than one light English cue in a short asset.
- Never make English required for understanding the message.
- If the English phrase makes the message feel forced, remove it.

## Phrases To Avoid

- Oferta irrepetible por tiempo mega limitado
- La mejor plataforma del pais
- Garantizado al 100%
- Servicio instantaneo siempre
- Tus problemas terminan hoy mismo
- No vuelvas a preocuparte nunca
- Te cambiamos la vida
- Somos numero uno en todo

## Good Vs Bad Examples

### Clients

Good:

- "Encuentra ayuda confiable para resolverlo sin complicarte de mas."

Bad:

- "La plataforma definitiva que revolucionara tu forma de contratar servicios."

Why:

- The good version is clear and useful.
- The bad version is inflated and abstract.

### Professionals

Good:

- "Muestra tu trabajo a clientes serios y responde con una imagen mas profesional."

Bad:

- "Multiplica tus ingresos ya con la plataforma que todos aman."

Why:

- The good version respects the pro.
- The bad version feels manipulative and ungrounded.

### Businesses

Good:

- "Organiza servicios recurrentes con un flujo mas claro y proveedores confiables."

Bad:

- "Escala tus operaciones con una disrupcion total del mantenimiento."

Why:

- The good version sounds operational and credible.
- The bad version sounds like generic startup jargon.

## Messaging Guardrails

- Be direct before being clever.
- Use warmth without losing authority.
- Turn trust into proof, not decoration.
- Make every CTA feel safe and specific.
- If a sentence sounds like it belongs to any brand, rewrite it.
